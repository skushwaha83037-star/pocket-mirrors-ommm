Privacy Policy

What Info We Grab

If you fill out a form, we get your name and email.

We also keep an eye on how folks use the site – stuff like IP address, browser type, and which pages get the most love. It's all anonymous.

What We Do With It

We use your info to answer your questions.

It also helps us make the site better.

If you sign up for updates, we'll send them your way.

Cookies

We might use cookies to remember what you like or see how people use the site. You can turn them off in your browser if you want.

Sharing Your Stuff

We don't sell your data. We might use reliable services (like hosting or analytics) that respect your privacy.

You're In Charge

You can unsubscribe from emails whenever you want.

If you want us to delete your info, just ask.
